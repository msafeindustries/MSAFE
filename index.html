How It Works

From tank to switch, in four steps.

01

Sensing

Magnetic floats continuously track the water level inside your tank without any moving electronics in contact with water.

02

Decision

The controller compares live level data against your set high and low points to decide whether the pump should run.

03

Switching

The relay engages or cuts power to the pump motor, with a built-in delay to prevent rapid on/off cycling.

04

Protection

If a dry-run or overflow condition is detected, AQUARUN overrides the cycle and shuts the pump down immediately.

01

Mount the Unit

Fix the controller near your pump switch or distribution board, within reach of the tank wiring.

02

Place the Floats

Position the magnetic floats at your desired high and low water marks inside the tank.

03

Wire the Pump

Connect the pump supply through the AQUARUN relay following the included wiring guide.

04

Power On

Switch on the mains supply — AQUARUN begins automatic operation immediately, no configuration needed.
